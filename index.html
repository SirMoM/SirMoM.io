#Plastober Challange 2020

I participated in the #plastober Challange.
In this article I want to share my thoughts and give insights to some of these pictures I drew.
The gist ist every day there is a promt and you “have to” draw something based on that word. This promt list had some extras: Every wendsday was a day off. And Week 2, 3 and 4 had superthemes.
I'll append all tweets for the prompts at the images. You can follow me but don't expect any images in the near furture!

The list of prompts is available here:

Decided to drop #inktober, I guess some of you know the reasons. Still love that challenge though, so I made my own list: #plastober - feel free to join and share your progress! #noinktober #artchallenge #drawtober #pentober #artistontwitter #inktoberprompts #promptlist #drawing pic.twitter.com/gkmgVmQePd
