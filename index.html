#Plastober Challange 2020

I participated in the #plastober Challange.
In this article I want to share my thoughts and give insights to some of these pictures I drew.
The gist ist every day there is a promt and you “have to” draw something based on that word. This promt list had some extras: Every wendsday was a day off. And Week 2, 3 and 4 had superthemes.
I'll append all tweets for the prompts at the images. You can follow me but don't expect any images in the near furture!

The list of prompts is available here:

Decided to drop #inktober, I guess some of you know the reasons. Still love that challenge though, so I made my own list: #plastober - feel free to join and share your progress! #noinktober #artchallenge #drawtober #pentober #artistontwitter #inktoberprompts #promptlist #drawing pic.twitter.com/gkmgVmQePd

— Stefan Große Halbuer (@grossehalbuer) September 8, 2020

Day 01: HEART

I bought myself aseprite for this challange. So day 1 was the first time using this programm. I had a lot to learn...
But working with aseprite was a bliss and using it was quite intuitive. But still I started small.
I like this in perticular because I can use it as an emoji which is nice.

Tweet!

Day 02: MOTH

Still not so familiar with aseprite I tried something more difficult. I started with rough shapes and refind it step by step.

Tweet!

Day 03: DEADLY

I did not have great idea and the only thing I could think of was a skull. But after experimenting a bit a came up with a little animation. Plus now I got a skull emoji aswell.

Tweet!

Day 04: PRISON

This day I had an idea which was somewhat obvious. Nonetheless, I think I turned out great. The thumb is quite janky but I like it.
And I made a stupid gif...

Tweet!

Day 05: QUEEN/KING

This week's supertheme was NATURE STRIKES BACK
(no that's not the Star Wars Movie).
I loved this supertheme. Having ideas according to this theme was the most fun out of the themes.
So the idea was a crown overgrown by some ivy. I think this image has potential, but I didn't have enough skill. I like the "main blue gem" and the "main red gem" also I love the small leafs, but the large leaf is awefull. All in all it is an OK image.

Tweet!

Day 06: DESERT

This is one of my favorit, if not the favorit image from the challange. It is simplistic but yet if checks all boxes. And the supertheme is worked in aswell.
It is simple and the contrast and colors works better than in my other images in this challange!

Tweet!

Day 07: Sparkling

I had a hard time comming up with something to draw with this prompt. In the end I wanted to draw a glittering / sparkling ocean.
The sparkling effect didn't work out as I planned, but I like the image nevertheless.

Tweet!

Day 09: FOX

“I tried so hard and got so far
But in the end it doesn't even look like a fox.”

I think this summerises it well. The proportions are of and some other things turned out not like I imagined. Tweet!

Day 10: WIND

I like this image it gives me a "lofi radio backgournd picture"-vibe.
I think the sky and the gradient turned out well. The stylised clouds add a certain falare to it.
The supertheme is worked in with the windmill gernerating "green" energy. Maybe I come back to this some thime and animate the windmills we'll see.

This is one of my favorites as well!

Tweet!

Day 11: FIGHTING

On this day I had zero creativity and no idea what to draw with the prompt FIGHTING, but i can still copy common methapors.
The resulting image is nice so I see this as an absolute win!

Tweet!

Day 12: BRIGHT

Day 12 marks the beginning of a new week and thus a new supertheme: HIGH TECH, LOW LIFE or in other words Cyberpunk. Well thinking about Cyberpunk and bright I thought about old retro themed highway with a bright, stylised sun.
The outcome was not as bright as I hoped.

Tweet!

Day 13: SCHOOL

Nothing to see here move on ...
Ok, this is by far the wors of all my images. You can't recognize it is a school and and the supertheme is also not worked in.
It just looks wonky. It isn't even filly on the image... Uff.

Tweet!

Day 14: MONEY

I had no great idea, so I drew the fallout money (even if fallout is technicly not cyberpunkt.. but I don't care). Because I had some spare time i drew the Bitcoin logo aswell, not sure why but it workes I guess. Neither turned out awesome but rather alright and the bottle cap isn't easy to recognize.

Tweet!

Day 16: LAMP

This one I like a lot. I has not really something to do with HIGH TECH, LOW LIFE, but the asthetics are great.
the Background is simple and clear with a nice gradient. The flickering light gives a nice athmoshere. And the transparent glass works better than I thought. The only thing which is bugging me is that the perspective isn't right for the one bus stop posts. I didn't see this while painting... Oh well it is still a nice picture/GIF.
